# Payroll Policy 2024 - First Half (Version 1.0)

## Company Payroll Policy Document
**Effective Period: January 1, 2024 - June 30, 2024**

### Overview
This document outlines the company's comprehensive payroll policy for the first half of 2024, including salary structures, benefits, deductions, and payment procedures.

### Salary Structure (H1 2024)
- Basic Salary: Core monthly compensation
- Housing Allowance: Up to 22% of basic salary (H1 2024 rate)
- Transportation Allowance: Fixed monthly amount based on position level
- Performance Bonus: Quarterly assessment-based bonus (10% cap in H1)

### Payment Schedule
- Salaries are paid on the 25th of each month
- If the 25th falls on a weekend or holiday, payment is made on the preceding business day
- Direct bank transfer is the standard payment method

### Deductions (H1 2024 Rates)
- Income Tax: Calculated according to government tax brackets (2024 Q1-Q2 rates)
- Health Insurance: 4.5% of gross salary (shared 50/50 with company)
- Retirement Fund: 7.5% of basic salary (company matches 100%)
- Social Security: As per government regulations

### Overtime Policy (H1 2024)
- Standard overtime rate: 1.5x regular hourly rate
- Weekend work: 1.8x regular hourly rate  
- Holiday work: 2.2x regular hourly rate
- Overtime must be pre-approved by direct supervisor
- Maximum 15 hours overtime per month

### Benefits (H1 2024)
- Annual leave: 20 working days per year
- Sick leave: 12 days per year with medical certificate
- Maternity/Paternity leave: As per local regulations
- Professional development allowance: $1,800 per year

### Tax Calculation (H1 2024)
- Tax brackets as defined in Tax Calculation Reference v3.1
- Standard deduction: $12,000 annually
- Marginal tax rates apply progressively

### Compliance
All payroll procedures comply with local labor laws and tax regulations. This policy is reviewed annually and updated as necessary.
