# Payroll Policy 2024

## Company Payroll Policy Document

### Overview
This document outlines the company's comprehensive payroll policy for the year 2024, including salary structures, benefits, deductions, and payment procedures.

### Salary Structure
- Basic Salary: Core monthly compensation
- Housing Allowance: Up to 25% of basic salary
- Transportation Allowance: Fixed monthly amount based on position level
- Performance Bonus: Quarterly assessment-based bonus

### Payment Schedule
- Salaries are paid on the 25th of each month
- If the 25th falls on a weekend or holiday, payment is made on the preceding business day
- Direct bank transfer is the standard payment method

### Deductions
- Income Tax: Calculated according to government tax brackets
- Health Insurance: 5% of gross salary (shared 50/50 with company)
- Retirement Fund: 8% of basic salary (company matches 100%)
- Social Security: As per government regulations

### Overtime Policy
- Standard overtime rate: 1.6x regular hourly rate
- Weekend work: 2x regular hourly rate
- Holiday work: 2.5x regular hourly rate
- Overtime must be pre-approved by direct supervisor

### Benefits
- Annual leave: 21 working days per year
- Sick leave: 14 days per year with medical certificate
- Maternity/Paternity leave: As per local regulations
- Professional development allowance: $2,000 per year

### Compliance
All payroll procedures comply with local labor laws and tax regulations. This policy is reviewed annually and updated as necessary.
